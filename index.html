CESS

CESS is computer engineering student society
Established to enhance the skills of students.
It's an organization build by computer science
students.CESS stands by the
motto-by the students, of the students and for the students.

Purpose:-
CESS purpose is to help students make their journey in GNDU
smooth and fruitful. It has various clubs to guide you. It
enlightens the path how to enrich your life as a student
and provides you with a great opportunity to network.

  • Placement Team
  • Tech Team
  • Public Relation Team
  • Promotion Team
  • Design Team
  • Fine Art Team
  • Literary Team

Tech Team

The Father of all other Teams of CESS . The Tech Team aims to promote creativity and to increase the technical know-how of each and every member. We will achieve these goals by conducting various interactive workshops and by hands-on fun practice sessions.

Literary Team

  • Literary team is a group of students interested in literature. It's a full fledge environment where a student can discuss about books, writings and can share insights on politics, life, nature and university issues.
  • Time to time CESS organizes a rich and diverse array of literary activities such as debates, declamation, group discussion, poetry writing, recitation, short story writing, quiz contests, interactive lecture or sessions. The Literary team focus to develop self-efficacy and confidence and would help to enhance the general knowledge base.
